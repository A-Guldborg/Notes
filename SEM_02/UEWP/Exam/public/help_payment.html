Payment

After a ride has ended, you will be led to the payment page where you can swipe to pay using your preferred payment method such as MobilePay or credit card!

If you don’t want to enter your payment information every ride, you can set up automatic payment from your profile.

Fill in your credit card informations which will be securely stored, and we will charge you for your rides automatically - It’s that easy!
